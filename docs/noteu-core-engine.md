# NoteU Core Engine

## Purpose

The NoteU Core Engine is the central intelligence system of the application.

Every note, review, AI analysis, and identity update passes through this engine.

Its purpose is to transform learning activities into measurable intellectual growth.

---

# Core Philosophy

Traditional note apps store information.

NoteU measures transformation.

The objective is not to collect knowledge.

The objective is to understand how knowledge changes a person over time.

---

# Complete Learning Pipeline

User learns something
        ↓
Creates a note
        ↓
AI analyzes the note
        ↓
Knowledge is categorized
        ↓
Concepts are extracted
        ↓
Knowledge Graph updates
        ↓
Review schedule generated
        ↓
Daily Review verifies retention
        ↓
Identity Score recalculated
        ↓
Learning Identity updated
        ↓
Dashboard personalized
        ↓
Recommendations generated

---

# Engine Modules

## 1. Input Engine

Receives:

- Notes
- Images
- Voice Notes
- PDF Highlights
- Imported Knowledge

Converts everything into structured learning objects.

---

## 2. AI Understanding Engine

Responsible for:

- Topic Detection
- Concept Extraction
- Knowledge Domains
- Summary Generation
- Difficulty Estimation

Output:

Structured knowledge.

---

## 3. Knowledge Graph Engine

Stores relationships between:

- Concepts
- Notes
- Domains
- Learning Sessions

Tracks how understanding grows.

---

## 4. Review Engine

Schedules reviews using:

- Spaced Repetition
- Confidence
- Recall History
- Review Streaks

Purpose:

Verify learning.

---

## 5. Identity Engine

Calculates:

- Identity Score
- Learning Identity
- Identity Level
- Growth Trend

This engine answers:

Who is this learner becoming?

---

## 6. Recommendation Engine

Uses every previous module.

Suggests:

- Next review
- Missing knowledge
- Related concepts
- Weak areas
- New learning paths

---

## 7. Analytics Engine

Measures

- Retention
- Consistency
- Learning Time
- Topic Distribution
- Knowledge Diversity
- Identity Progression

# Product Philosophy

The engine should never replace learning.

It should amplify it.

AI should not become the learner.

The user should.

AI exists only to reveal patterns humans cannot easily see.

---

# Success Metric

The engine succeeds when users can answer:

"I know more than I did last year."

"I understand more deeply."

"I can connect ideas I never connected before."

"I can clearly see who I'm becoming."
