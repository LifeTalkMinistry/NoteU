# NoteU Vision

## Mission
Help people discover who they are becoming through what they consistently learn.

## Vision
NoteU is not another note-taking application.

It is the world's first Learning Identity Platform that measures learning, retention, consistency, and knowledge connections to reveal a person's intellectual growth over time.

## Core Philosophy

Your notes show what you learned.

Your review shows what you retained.

Together, they reveal who you're becoming.

## Long-Term Goal

To become the personal operating system for lifelong learners.
