# First-Time User Experience

## Goal

The first experience should convince users that NoteU is not another note-taking app.

Within five minutes, users should understand one idea:

"I am building a learning identity."

---

# Step 1 — Welcome

Headline

Welcome to NoteU

Subtitle

The notes that reveal you.

Short explanation

Most note apps help you remember information.

NoteU helps you discover who you're becoming through what you consistently learn.

Button

Begin Your Journey

---

# Step 2 — Learning Profile

Ask only a few questions.

Examples

What are you currently learning?

Choose up to five.

- Science
- Business
- Finance
- Technology
- Psychology
- Faith
- Leadership
- Creativity
- Health
- Languages

Purpose

This is only the starting point.

Your real identity will come from your learning.

---

# Step 3 — First Note

Prompt

Write something you learned today.

Placeholder

Explain it in your own words.

Not copy and paste.

This immediately teaches users the philosophy of NoteU.

---

# Step 4 — AI Analysis

Animation

Analyzing your understanding...

The AI extracts:

- Topics
- Concepts
- Learning domains
- Related ideas

The user watches their first knowledge map appear.

---

# Step 5 — First Identity

Example

Learning Identity

Explorer

Identity Confidence

12%

Message

It's too early to know who you're becoming.

Keep learning.

We'll discover it together.

This avoids giving fake confidence too early.

---

# Step 6 — Daily Review

Introduce the Daily Review.

Explain that learning is verified through review, not just note creation.

# Product Philosophy

The first session should create curiosity.

The second session builds trust.

The third session creates habit.
