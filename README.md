# NoteU

> The notes that reveal you.

## Vision

NoteU is not just a note-taking app.

It is a learning identity platform that analyzes what users learn, what they review, and what they consistently retain.

Instead of simply storing notes, NoteU helps users discover who they are becoming through knowledge.

## Core Principle

Your notes show what you learned.

Your review shows what you retained.

Together, they reveal who you're becoming.

## Planned Features

- AI-powered note categorization
- Daily 30-minute review
- Learning Identity Profile
- Identity Score
- Knowledge combinations
- Learning analytics
- Progress tracking
- Cross-disciplinary insights
